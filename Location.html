Welcome to Our Campus

St. Lawrence Junior School is nestled in a serene, accessible, and secure neighborhood in Kabowa, Rubaga Division, Kampala. Our campus is designed to foster learning, safety, and community spirit. Whether you are a parent, student, or visitor, you'll find our location easy to reach and welcoming to all.

  • Safe, child-friendly environment
  • Easy access from major roads
  • Vibrant, supportive community

Why Visit Us?

  • Secure, gated campus with 24/7 security
  • Green, peaceful grounds for learning and play
  • Convenient school transport options
  • Friendly staff ready to assist you

Nearby Landmarks

  • Kabowa Catholic Church (2 min walk)
  • Kabowa Health Centre (5 min walk)
  • Nyanama Market (7 min drive)
  • Kabowa Police Post (5 min walk)
  • Nyanama Road (Main access road)

How to Reach Us

Multiple convenient ways to reach our campus

By Car

1

From Kampala city center, take Entebbe Road

2

Turn onto Nyanama Road at the Kabowa junction

3

Continue for 500 meters and turn onto Gabunga Road

4

Look for our signpost opposite Jackayz Secondary School

Estimated time: 15-20 minutes from city center

By Boda Boda

1

Find any boda boda stage in Nyanama or Kabowa

2

Ask for "St. Lawrence Junior School, Kabowa"

3

They will drop you directly at the school gate

Estimated time: 5-10 minutes from Nyanama

By Public Transport

1

Board a taxi to Nyanama from any major stage

2

Alight at Kabowa stage

3

Walk 5 minutes to the school gate

Estimated time: 20-30 minutes from city center

On Foot

1

From Kabowa Church Zone, follow the pedestrian path

2

Turn onto Gabunga Road

3

School entrance will be on your right

Estimated time: 10-15 minutes from Kabowa Church
