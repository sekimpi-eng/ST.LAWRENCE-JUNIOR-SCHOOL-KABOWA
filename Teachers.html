Continuous Professional Development

At St. Lawrence Junior School, we believe in nurturing our educators as much as our students. Our teachers are committed to lifelong learning, regularly participating in advanced workshops, seminars, and cutting-edge training programs. This dedication ensures they stay updated with the latest educational trends, innovative methodologies, and best practices, directly translating into dynamic, engaging, and highly effective learning experiences for our students.

  • Annual Teacher Training Retreats: Immersive sessions focused on advanced pedagogy, curriculum enrichment, and well-being.
  • Workshops on Digital Learning: Equipping teachers with skills to integrate technology seamlessly into classrooms for enhanced engagement.
  • Peer-to-Peer Mentoring: A collaborative program where experienced educators guide and support newer staff, fostering a culture of shared growth.
  • Certification in Child Psychology & Safety: Ensuring our staff are well-versed in student welfare, mental health, and creating a secure learning environment.
