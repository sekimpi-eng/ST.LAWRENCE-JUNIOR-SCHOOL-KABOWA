About Us

About Us

An Innovative Approach to Learning

St. Lawrence Junior School is a premier mixed day and boarding primary school that combines academic excellence with holistic development. Established in 2010, we offer both nursery and primary education, maintaining our commitment to fostering a nurturing environment for all students.

Academic Excellence

Consistently high performance in national examinations

Inclusive Environment

Welcoming students from diverse backgrounds

Holistic Development

Focus on character building and life skills

Expert Faculty

Dedicated and experienced teaching staff

Located in the vibrant Rubaga Division within the Kabowa Church Zone, St. Lawrence Junior School is rooted in a strong Catholic tradition while welcoming students and parents from diverse religious backgrounds. Our mission is to provide an innovative and inclusive education that prepares students to become confident, compassionate, and globally-minded individuals.
